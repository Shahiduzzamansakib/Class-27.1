What is the role of the web designer?

As a web designer, you are responsible for big-picture decisions, like the menus listed on the site, and smaller details, like which fonts, colors, and graphics to use. A web designer creates the layout and design of a website. In simple terms, a website designer makes a site look good.

Is web design a good career?

With more people launching websites, the demand for knowledgeable and skilled web designers is high. In fact, according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the job outlook for web developers and digital designers is expected to grow by 16 percent between 2022 and 2032 alone.
